Our Process

<

We Understand

Listening to you is our priority to gain a clear understanding of your needs, budget, and timeline.

Brainstorm

A creativity exercise with our team to generate unique ideas based on research.

Ideate

We form a slider with options related to your interests, alongside a venue visit.

Present

We meet in person or online to give a detailed presentation of your Big Day.

Revision

Based on your feedback, necessary changes are made, and the final version is presented.

Commercials & SOW

We share a proposal with commercials and Scope of Work, leading to negotiation and finalization.

Design & Development

A 3D layout is shared for stage, seating, and decorations to help visualize your Big Day.

Final Review

A virtual walkthrough is done to ensure the design is finalized and shop-ready.

Execute

The Big Daydreams are brought to life!
